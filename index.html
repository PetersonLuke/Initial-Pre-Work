Who Are They

Edwin Pepping, Albert Mampre, Earnest Oats, and Eugene Roe served as Combat Medics attached to Easy Company, 2nd Battalion, 506th Parachute Infantry Regiment, 101st Airborne Division, also known as “Band of Brothers”. Their combat tour brought them through some of the hardest fighting during the war, but they were armed only with basic medical equipment. The men of Easy company refered to these Medics as the "Band-aid Bandits".

Pepping, Mempre, and Oats are the three Medics that started at Camp Toccao with the forming of the company. Mempre received an infection before the jump into Normandy and was replaced by Roe.

During the invasion of Normandy, the aircraft Oats was aboard was shot down killing all aboard leaving Roe and Pepping as the Medics. Pepping was injured well in Normandy and got replaced by Spina.

Mampre rejoining the company and continued on with Roe and in Easy Company for the rest of the war.
